Understanding Web Design by Jeffrey Zeldman—An Event Apart Video from Jeffrey Zeldman on Vimeo.

Jeffrey co-founded and publishes A Book Apart (“brief books for people who make websites”), responsible for such influential titles as Responsive Web Design by Ethan Marcotte and Mobile First by Luke Wroblewski. He’s a faculty member on the MFA, Interaction Design program at School of Visual Arts, New York, and the host of weekly internet radio program The Big Web Show (“Everything Web That Matters”), twice named Podcast of the Year by net magazine. In 2012, Jeffrey was the first designer inducted in the SXSW Interactive Hall of Fame.
